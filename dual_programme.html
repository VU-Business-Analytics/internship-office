Dual Master's Programme in Business Analytics

The dual master’s programme combines part-time employment with part-time study. Students work in a relevant position while pursuing their Master’s in Business Analytics. In recognition of their professional experience, students earn 12 EC (credit points) for their work. The external graduation project (Master Project Business Analytics) can also be completed at the same company—an option most dual students choose.

Admission Requirements

Admission to the dual master’s programme is open to students with a Bachelor’s degree in Business Analytics. Students with a different academic background—such as Mathematics, Econometrics, or Computer Science—may be admitted on a case-by-case basis. For questions about eligibility, please contact Joost Berkhout.

Please note: Admission also depends on securing suitable employment. Unfortunately, due to IND regulations, the dual master’s programme is not available to non-EU students.

Dual Work Period Proposal

If you have found a suitable dual job position, you are encouraged to submit your Dual Work Period proposal to Annemieke van Goor,, preferably before June 1, 2025.
